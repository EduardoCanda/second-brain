## Tags recomendadas
- `#concept`
- `#tutorial`
- `#reference`
- `#troubleshooting`
- `#best-practice`
- Tags de domínio: `#linux`, `#kubernetes`, `#network`, `#security`

## Padrão por tipo
- Fundamentals: `#concept`
- Guides: `#tutorial`
- Deep Dives: `#concept #architecture`
- Labs: `#tutorial #hands-on`
- Playbooks: `#operations #reference`

## Exemplo
```md
Tags: #tutorial #kubernetes #network
```
